FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions before reaching out? We've answered the most common ones below.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timelines vary based on the complexity and scope of your project. A simple brochure site typically takes 2-3 weeks, while a fully custom web application or e-commerce platform can range from 4-8 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline during your free consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We proudly serve clients across 20+ countries including the USA, UK, UAE, Australia, and Singapore. All communication and project management is handled seamlessly through online platforms, so geography is never a barrier.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including bank transfer, UPI, credit/debit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for international clients. Projects are typically structured with a milestone-based payment schedule for complete transparency.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer flexible maintenance and support packages to keep your digital assets running smoothly after launch. This includes security updates, performance monitoring, content edits, and feature additions - tailored to your needs.

Can I see your portfolio before hiring?

Of course! You're welcome to browse our full portfolio on our Projects page. You'll find case studies, live links, and detailed breakdowns of work across various industries and service categories.
